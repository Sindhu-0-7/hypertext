World of bikes

Bikes, also known as bicycles, are two-wheeled vehicles powered by human effort. They are a popular mode of transportation, recreation, and exercise.

  • Road Bikes
  • Mountain Bikes
  • Hybrid Bikes
  • Electric Bikes (E-Bikes)
  • Commuter Bikes
  • Folding Bikes

bike components

Frame: The main structure, usually made of aluminum, carbon fiber, steel, or titanium.
Wheels: Comprised of rims, spokes, hubs, and tires; vary in size depending on the type of bike.
Drivetrain: Includes the pedals, cranks, chain, gears, and derailleurs, which work together to move the bike forward.
Brakes: Can be rim brakes, disc brakes, or drum brakes, used to slow down or stop the bike.
Handlebars: Vary in shape and size, influencing the rider's posture and control.
Saddle: The seat, designed for comfort during rides.
Suspension: Found in mountain bikes, with front (fork) or full (front and rear) suspension to absorb shocks from rough terrain.

safety tips

Helmet: Always wear a helmet to protect your head in case of a fall or collision.
Lights and Reflectors: Use them to increase visibility, especially at night or in low-light conditions.
Follow Traffic Rules: Obey all traffic signals and signs, and ride in the direction of traffic.
Be Aware: Stay alert to your surroundings, including other vehicles, pedestrians, and road conditions.
